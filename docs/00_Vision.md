# MISO: The World's Most Luxurious Pan-Asian Dining Platform

## The Vision
MISO is not a food delivery application. It is the future of fine dining discovery and experience. Imagine if Apple, Aman Resorts, Airbnb Experiences, the Michelin Guide, and Muji collaborated to create a dining platform. The result is MISO.

MISO is a beautifully curated Asian lifestyle magazine where every restaurant tells a story, every dish has heritage, and every interaction creates anticipation. Every screen should feel calm, intentional, timeless, and sophisticated. It should make users feel they are beginning a memorable journey through the diverse culinary cultures of Asia.

## Core Philosophy
1. **Editorial Over Transactional**: Replace generic listings with immersive collections (e.g., "Taste of Kyoto", "Hidden Gems of Seoul", "Tokyo After Dark"). Use cinematic photography, elegant layouts, and crafted storytelling.
2. **The Culinary Passport**: Progress through the app is a luxury travel journal. Every country explored is a beautifully designed passport page. Every restaurant visited is a collectible stamp.
3. **Cultural Adaptability**: The app subtly adapts to the culture of the selected cuisine (e.g., Sakura and bamboo for Japan, Jade and silk for China, Celadon for Korea) while preserving the overarching MISO identity.
4. **Cinematic Restaurant Pages**: Restaurants are luxury destinations. Present them through their philosophy, chef introductions, and architecture before ever showing a menu.
5. **Editorial Menus**: Dishes are presented with ingredient sourcing, preparation techniques, regional history, and chef commentary.
6. **Ceremonial Reservations**: The booking process feels like reserving a luxury hotel, complete with ambient lighting previews, seating maps, and elegant visual feedback.
7. **Refined Visual Language**: Abundant whitespace, ultra-thin premium line icons, handcrafted textures (rice paper, bamboo, ceramics), depth over decoration, and a cohesive 8-point grid.
8. **Artisanal Motion Design**: Flowing ink animations, gently drifting particles, soft steam, subtle parallax, and fluid 60fps haptic-inspired micro-interactions.
9. **AI Concierge**: Natural AI integration that recommends experiences based on mood, season, weather, and travel preferences.
10. **Premium Empty States**: Every loading screen, offline state, and confirmation animation is treated with the same care as primary screens.
